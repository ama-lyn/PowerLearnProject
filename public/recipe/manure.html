In this recipe, we will focus on manure, one of the most abundant and readily available organic wastes on any farm.


Manure is not just waste...

It's a powerful resource that can fuel sustainability and productivity on the farm.

The process:

Collect manure, preferably 5 buckets, and transfer it to your mixing area. This can be a large container or pit where the manure will be mixed with water.

Mix the manure with water (at a ratio of 1:1), ensuring to remove any stones, grass, or other debris from the mixture. This will help create a uniform consistency.

Channel the manure-water mixture into your biodigester or composting bin. A biodigester is a sealed container that helps break down the organic matter and convert it into biogas.

Seal the biodigester or cover the composting bin and let the biogas production process begin. This process can take a few weeks depending on the size of your system and environmental factors.

Estimated Time Approximately 4-6 weeks for biogas production.
